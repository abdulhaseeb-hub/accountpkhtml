Inbound Call Center Process Flow

We incorporate custom scripts, optimized floor management, and data analytics to offer consolidated inbound call center solutions to enterprises. Our agents strategically respond to inbound calls using the following process -

 

01. Engage The Caller

After a customer sends a request to the call center, our agent engages with them according to the pre-decided script.

 

02. Note Down Requirement

Our agent talks to the customer to determine the reason for the inbound call.

 

03. Determine Solution

After noting the reason for the call, our agent determines the best course of action that will ensure customer satisfaction.

 

04. Offer Support

Once our agent maps out the right course of action, they offer support to the customer in the form of answering queries, resolving issues, and more.

Why Offshore Inbound Customer Support?

Answering machines with pre-recorded messages are cold, inaccurate, and impersonal. Recent research has shown that customers prefer live telephone answering services instead. Having calls answered by a live representative in real-time will shoot up your sales and generate brand loyalty.

However, maintaining well-organized call center requires huge administrative setup. Outsource a non-core business function like inbound customer support and save a great deal of time, effort, and money. That way, you can -

  • Drastically reduce operational and infrastructural costs
  • Achieve higher service quality with dedicated and experienced resources
  • Benefit from an increase in customer satisfaction
  • Maximize revenue and productivity by quickly resolving customer queries
  • Easily expand your network and leverage on state-of-the-art infrastructure and technology of your vendor
